Meet DoubleCloud AI-Insights based on GPT-4

May 25, 2023
5 mins to read

We are excited to announce the release of a powerful new feature that will revolutionize the way you gain insights from your data. Introducing AI-Insights, a cutting-edge addition to DoubleCloud Visualization that harnesses the power of ChatGPT to provide you with ready-made conclusions from your charts with just one click.

Instant conclusionsInstant conclusions

With ChatGPT integrated into DoubleCloud Visualization, you can now extract valuable insights from your data effortlessly. Simply click on your charts, and AI-Insights will analyze the underlying data, automatically recognize the subject matter, and provide you with a precise and unbiased summary.

Multilingual supportMultilingual support

Language is no barrier when it comes to AI-Insights. This powerful feature is supported in seven different languages, allowing you to explore and understand your data regardless of the language in which it is presented.

Time-saving efficiencyTime-saving efficiency

Say goodbye to spending hours manually analyzing data and deriving conclusions. You don’t even need to write a prompt, as it’s already a part of a feature.

Unbiased summariesUnbiased summaries

AI-Insights leverages the advanced capabilities of ChatGPT to provide unbiased summaries of your data. By removing human bias from the equation, you can make objective and informed decisions based on the insights generated by the system.

Below you can see an example of how AI-Insights provides a quick overview of a large table of marketing campaign performance data, which may not be easy for a human to understand swiftly.

We believe that AI-Insights will be a game-changer for data analysis, empowering you to extract valuable insights from your visualizations effortlessly. With its integration into DoubleCloud Visualization, you now have a powerful tool at your disposal to accelerate your decision-making process.
